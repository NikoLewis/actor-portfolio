Tyechia Smith

From the track to the stage and into the cosmic future,
Tyechia Smith embodies innovative reinvention.

A natural on stage and screen, she is eager to illuminate your project with her versatile talents. Whether you need a dystopian future matriarch, an aloof time-traveling diplomat, or a stoic bio-engineered space pirate with a heart of gold, she's primed and ready for activation. With a powerful blend of athleticism, emotional depth, and a passion for dynamic storytelling, she brings an otherworldly and unforgettable presence to every role.

Once upon a time...
On a planet in a galaxy, far away...

Transitioning from a celebrated track and field career—including induction into her college's Hall of Fame and modeling in Adias' Campaigns—to the captivating world of acting. From Jersey City to Atlanta, her journey has been marked by a relentless pursuit of artistic expression.

Driven by a deep desire to explore the human experience, Tyechia immersed herself in the rigorous two-and-a-half-year program at the prestigious William Esper Studio in New York City. She mastered the Meisner technique, honing her ability to embody characters with authentic emotional depth. This training not only refined her acting skills but also expanded her empathy, allowing her to delve into the nuances of period pieces, portray characters with distinct motivations, and use physicality to convey palpable emotions.

In early 2024, Tyechia made the move to Atlanta, a city brimming with creative energy, to fully dedicate herself to her acting career. While the recent industry strike presented unforeseen challenges, her commitment remains unwavering. Her portfolio reflects a focused dedication to her craft, featuring appearances in Nike commercials, student films, and a foundation built through community theater.

Tyechia's versatility shines through in her range, encompassing dramatic roles, comedic performances, and a particular affinity for portraying strong, older characters. Her love for science fiction, especially time-traveling adventures that ignite the imagination, fuels her desire to collaborate with visionary directors like Christopher Nolan. She was also profoundly impacted by Viola Davis's raw and powerful performance in "How to Get Away with Murder," as well as Nolan's "Memento," "Interstellar," and "Inception."

Recognizing the need for stability in the entertainment industry and the ability to use digital platforms as a storytelling medium, Tyechia, with her characteristic blend of passion and practicality, is also developing her skills in web development. This blend of creative passion and practical foresight exemplifies her drive and determination.

Tyechia Smith is an actor who embraces the unexpected, thrives on challenge, and brings a unique blend of athleticism, emotional depth, and intellectual curiosity to every role and project. She is ready to bring her unique perspective to the stage and screens large and small, and leave a lasting impression across digital mediums.
